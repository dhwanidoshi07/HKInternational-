H.K.INTERNATIONAL is one of the leading Manufacturer, Exporter, Supplier & Stockist for all type of FASTENERS & GASKETS and Hardware Items in all grades of material. We are established to Design and Manufacture Stud Bolts, Nuts, Foundation Bolts, U-Clamps, Collar Bolts, Carriage Bolts, Scaffolding Clamps & Accessories & all type of Special Customized Fasteners & Gaskets like Spiral Wound, ASB./ Non Asb. Ring Type etc. for Industrial, Refineries, and Petrochemicals Plants and on shore- off shore installations.

Quality Assurance

With 22 years of rich Technical knowledge (Since 1988), we have been manufacturing these products with carefully selected material of the finest metallurgical quality, the control of the production and materials by stringent applications of ‘Quality Assurance’ are strictly adhered to.
